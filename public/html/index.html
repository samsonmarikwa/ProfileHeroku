Bio

I am a passionate developer who started off as a COBOL programmer in a mainframe environment. I am now into web (cloud) application development. In between these roles, I moved into systems administration supporting a network of about a 100 users in a Windows environment. In that role, I continued to write small programs in Clipper to extract data from the main systems to generate special reports. After that, I moved into application software implementation setting up Infor FMS SunSystems to address business requirements in financials and order fulfilment. In this role, there were occasions when I had to write programs in Java to enable interfaces to other systems.

My expertise is in the MERN web application development framework.

I am a detail-oriented architect keen on maximizing productivity through aggressive automation. I always want to explore an opportunity to computerize a process to increase productivity and let machines work for humankind.

The internet has brought so much opportunities to enhance productivity in that the client can transact at any place and time of their choice and as organizations, resources are redeployed to develop further products and services to meet the ever growing needs of clients.

My expertise and experience enable me to take up the role of a full stack web developer.
