About Me

I am a detail-oriented Web Application Architect keen on eliminating waste in resources through process automation and low-cost ubiquitous technology. I am customer-centric, without a happy customer we have no business being here. My desired achievement in any project is to have a product that provides an aesthetic interface that has the impact of engaging the customer. The website should speak to the customer as to the quality of products and services at his or her disposal as it provides the first and continuous point of contact between our company and the customer. The customer should be able to access products and services from our organization without travelling to or calling our offices. That should enhance productivity both sides in that the customer can transact at any place and time of their choice and we as an organization, our resources are freed to concentrate on developing our products and services to meet the ever growing demands of our clients.

My skills and experiences enable me to take up the role of a full stack web developer.
